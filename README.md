📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a 360-degree view of institutional financial health, customer demographics, and loan portfolio performance. It is designed to help stakeholders monitor income trends, assess credit risks, and understand the relationship between employment status and financial stability.

🚀 Key Features
Multi-Page Navigation: Separate views for Financial Overviews, Loan & Credit Insights, and Customer Demographics.
Dynamic Simulations: An interactive Interest Rate Simulation to project EMI changes.
Trend Analysis: Year-over-Year (YOY) income and expense tracking (2021–2025).
Advanced Filtering: Slicers for Employment Status, Region, Loan Type, and Customer ID.

📊 Dashboard Breakdowns
1. Financial Overview
Focuses on high-level KPIs and long-term financial trends.
Key Metrics: Total Income ($130.60M), Total Savings ($7.90bn), and Total Expenses ($78.45M).
Income vs. Expenses: A comparative area chart showing growth from 2021 through 2025.
Savings Analysis: A bar chart revealing that Employed individuals contribute the highest share of savings ($4.7bn).
Performance Gauges: Visual tracking of YOY Income Growth and Expense targets.

2. Loan & Credit Insights
Deep dive into the loan portfolio and repayment capabilities.
Loan Distribution: Analysis of loan types (Home, Education, Business, Car) showing a nearly even split (approx. 25% each).
Geographic Spread: A tree map visualizing loan amounts across Europe, Asia, North America, Africa, and other regions.
Risk Metrics: Monitoring the Loan-to-Income Ratio (currently at 2.07) and total monthly EMI ($100.29M).
What-If Analysis: Includes a simulation tool to adjust interest rates and see real-time impact on EMIs for specific users.

3. Customer Demographics
Understanding the 'Who' behind the data.
Age Distribution: High concentration of customers in the 50–60 age bracket.
Regional Credit Scores: Average credit scores are consistently stable across regions, hovering around 575.
Gender Analysis: A balanced view of savings and loan amounts between Male and Female customers.
Education Impact: Visualizes average income levels across different education tiers (High School to PhD).

🛠️ Tech Stack
Tool: Power BI Desktop
Data Source: (SQL Server / Excel / CSV )
Data Modeling: Star Schema with dedicated Measure tables.
DAX Functions: Used for YOY Growth, Interest Simulations, and Dynamic Ranking (Top N).

💡 Business Insights
Demographic Target: The 60+ age group represents the largest customer segment, suggesting a need for retirement-focused financial products.
Financial Stability: Despite high expenses in 2022-2024, there is a projected stabilization in 2025.
Loan Portfolio: The portfolio is well-diversified across loan types, reducing the risk of a downturn in any single sector (e.g., real estate).
